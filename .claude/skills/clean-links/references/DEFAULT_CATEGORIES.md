# Default Categories for Newsletter

These are the 5 default categories proposed to the user:

1. **Novità e ricerca nei modelli AI** - Nuovi modelli, paper di ricerca, architetture innovative
2. **Agentic AI** - Agenti autonomi, sistemi multi-agente, orchestrazione
3. **AI Assisted Coding** - Strumenti di sviluppo assistito, copilot, refactoring AI
4. **Business e società** - Impatto sociale, regulamentazione, mercato del lavoro
5. **Robotica e Physical AI** - Robotica, computer vision, embodied AI

## Usage
Always propose these categories first in Step 4. The user can confirm them or provide alternatives.

## Category Guidelines
- **Novità e ricerca**: New model releases, research papers, novel architectures
- **Agentic AI**: Autonomous agents, multi-agent systems, orchestration frameworks
- **AI Assisted Coding**: Development tools, copilots, AI-powered refactoring
- **Business e società**: Market impact, regulation, workforce changes, business applications
- **Robotica e Physical AI**: Robotics, computer vision, embodied intelligence, hardware
